# Holding POKT

$POKT can be used in most modern EVM or Solana wallets, including:

* [Metamask](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/metamask/nkbihfbeogaeaoehlefnkodbefgpgknn?hl=en)
* [Trust Wallet](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/trust-wallet/egjidjbpglichdcondbcbdnbeeppgdph?hl=en)
* [Coinbase Wallet](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/coinbase-wallet-extension/hnfanknocfeofbddgcijnmhnfnkdnaad?hl=en)
* [Rabby Wallet](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/rabby-wallet/acmacodkjbdgmoleebolmdjonilkdbch?hl=en)
* [Solflare](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/solflare-wallet/bhhhlbepdkbapadjdnnojkbgioiodbic?hl=en)
* [Phantom](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/phantom/bfnaelmomeimhlpmgjnjophhpkkoljpa?hl=en)

In order to [stake a node](../pokt-rewards/stake-pokt.md) with your $POKT, you need to hold $POKT on POKT Network, POKT's native Tendermint based blockchain.

To hold $POKT on POKT Network here are our available wallets:

* [NodeWallet](./#nodewallet)
* [Pocket Wallet](./#pocket-wallet)
* [Ledger](https://app.gitbook.com/o/-MVU655bEpKMcmpTxlOG/s/sC7vRmj1fRzIvz4vDUe3/)

Once Shannon is deployed to mainnet, POKT holders will have access to all Cosmos wallets, e.g., [Keplr](https://www.keplr.app/) and [Leap](https://www.leapwallet.io/).
